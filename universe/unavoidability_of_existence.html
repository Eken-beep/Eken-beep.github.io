This ties in to the sentiment of thanking some higher power when something goes your way. Theres no shot that if god is sentient like us in some way, that he would give a flying fuck about whether you lost some football game or whatever first world problems people pray to god about nowadays. But one thing is for sure, god does not care if he created you or not. He probably has more important shit to do.

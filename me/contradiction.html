There is a fundamental contradiction I've found in my way of seeing the world

I have such hate for authoritarian actions by the people in power. I desire freedom for myself.

Yet I know what people do when they obtain this freedom.

Freedom of tax, the unfortunate now starve.

Freedom of law, I will now be ravaged by crime.

Freedom from shitty corporate practices, the companies now cease.

But when not given these freedoms I feel the system is unjust. I feel as though everyone should have the right to decide for themselves what is right or wrong. But that does not lead to everyone treating everyone well, because people are stupid. I know I would treat everyone with respect even when given total freedom, or would I?

My moral compass would tell me that giving others this freedom I desire is the only right I could do. Yet I would not want my peers to live with the consequences it gives.

What if I could live in freedom, nay anarchy, but everyone else has to live under authority. But that does still not make this gut feeling I have go away, that I am doing someone wrong when not sharing my freedom with others. However I know they will be better of under authority than in freedom, I've seen it.

I will keep on living with this contradiction for know. Wishing for a better future.
